What is Advance Space Camp?
The Advance Space Camp is an entertaining week-long camp held by NASA where different projects are created and a variety of simulators and attractions are carried out, all with the goal of teaching something different regarding astronaut training. This is for ages 15 to 18 which is also funny because of the age difference but how social it becomes to live with the same people day and night, considering that we also share rooms between 7 people. Informative presentations, creation of rockets with parachutes, simulators, planetarium, museum visit, diving, etc. They are only 1% of everything we do. These with many reactions, jokes, funny accidents, stress, amazement, a real roller coaster of emotions when exploring and learning so much in a week about such a big topic. .
My group
My team: My team was very entertaining, we were like 14, different personalities, ages, ideas but that didn't stop the week from being pure jokes between everyone, every moment we became united both in group work and only in meal times, I didn't think that in 1 week strangers could become friends as such incredible people. From day to night we couldn't wake up but I think that was the fun, both support and jokes were inevitable in each activity.
Where is the camp?
It is in Huntsville, Alabama in the United States. Hard to miss with the size and number of buildings in the area from the ´´habitats´´ buildings where we slept, to the planetarium, the dining room, the building with the classes where we had experiments, debates and presentations.
