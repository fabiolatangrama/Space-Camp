Como fue mi experiencia?

Mi experiencia era muy bonita y cara. Incluso conoci nueva gente.Y incluso me diverti muchisimo pero lo malo es que el huevo sabia a cerote.Pero venci el huevocerote con hash browns y waffles.La cama era dura como una piedra y habia un chamaco que no me dejaba dormir.El mae se reia toda la noche y una noche hizo una chilena y lo regañaron.Lanzamos choetes y nadamos en una piscina y la piscina estaba hirbiendo.En serio creame de verdad pero en serio estaba HIRVIENDO!!!!!!!!.Tambien habia una atraccion llamada "G force" y aceleraba a 3 fuerzas G.Y otra atraccion llamada "Moon shot" que subia rapido y bajaba lento pero lo malo es que no estaba funcionando.Qieren escuchar mas?Ok, tambien habia un planetario llamado "Intutive Planetarium" habia una cafeteria que no vendia cafe sino ICEEs, palomitas,dulces,etc.Y ahi adentro simulaba un viaje a todo los planetas incluso la luna y vimos "el borde del universo"Y mi compañero de la escuela Nicolas Meseguer viajo conmigo y los dos nos divertimos muchisimo.

How was my experience? My experience was very nice and expensive. I even met new people. And I even had a lot of fun but the bad thing is that the egg tasted like shit. But I beat the shitty egg with hash browns and waffles. The bed was hard as a rock and there was a kid who wouldn't let me sleep. The guy laughed all night and one night he did a Chilean and they scolded him. We threw rockets and swam in a pool and the pool was boiling. Seriously believe me but it was seriously BOILING!!!!!!!!. There was also a ride called "G force" and it accelerated to 3 G forces. And another ride called "Moon shot" that went up fast and came down slowly but the bad thing is that it wasn't working. Do you want to hear more? Ok, there was also a planetarium called "Intuitive Planetarium" there was a cafeteria that didn't sell coffee but ICEEs, popcorn, candy, etc. And in there they simulated a trip to all the planets including the moon and We saw "The Edge of the Universe" and my school friend Nicolas Meseguer traveled with me and we both had a lot of fun.
